Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I start playing D&D on D&D Zone?

Getting started is easy! Simply create an account, choose your character, and join a campaign. If you're new, you might want to start with our beginner's guide.

Do I need to own any D&D books?

While owning the books can enhance your gameplay, D&D Zone provides all the necessary resources and tools you need to play online.

Can I join a game that's already in progress?

Yes, you can join games in progress, but it's up to the Dungeon Master (DM) to accept new players into their campaign. We recommend communicating with the DM first.

Is D&D Zone free to use?

D&D Zone offers both free and premium features. The basic functionality is free, but you can access more advanced features with a subscription.
